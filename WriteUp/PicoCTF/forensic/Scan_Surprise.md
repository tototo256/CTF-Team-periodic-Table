```
I've gotten bored of handing out flags as text. Wouldn't it be cool if they were an image instead?
You can download the challenge files here:
challenge.zip
```

`challenge.zip`

2. 表示されたQRコードを読み取るとフラグが表示される
3. フラグ picoCTF{p33k_@_b00_d4ca652e}
